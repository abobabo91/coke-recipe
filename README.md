# Lab Coatz Lab-Cola (Chemically-Matched Coca-Cola Replica)

## Final Soda Formula (Version 111B)

**Per 1 liter of finished soda**
- 104 g sugar (sucrose)
- 1 mL Flavor Solution A (alcohol-based)
- 10 mL Flavor Solution B (water-based)
- Cold carbonated water, to 1 liter total volume

---

## Flavor Solution A (Alcohol-Based, “7X”)

### Step 1: Prepare the flavor oil blend
Combine:
- 45.8 mL lemon oil
- 36.5 mL lime oil
- 8.0 mL tea tree oil (Melaleuca alternifolia)
- 4.5 mL cassia cinnamon oil
- 2.7 mL nutmeg oil
- 1.2 mL sweet orange oil
- 0.7 mL coriander oil
- 0.6 mL α-fenchol

Let age 24–48 hours, sealed.

### Step 2: Dilute
- Take 20 mL of flavor oil
- Dilute to 1 liter using food-grade 95% ethanol

---

## Flavor Solution B (Water-Based)

### Ingredients
- 185 mL Durkee caramel color
- 160 g vegetable glycerin
- 45 mL 85% phosphoric acid
- 10 g wine tannin
- 9.65 g caffeine
- 10 mL 5% vinegar
- 10 mL vanilla extract (or 0.95 g/L vanillin solution)
- Water to 1 liter

### Preparation
1. Heat ~200 mL water
2. Add ingredients one at a time, fully dissolving each
3. Dilute to exactly 1 liter with water

---

## Making the Soda (1 Liter)

### Step 1: Sugar Syrup
- Dissolve 104 g sugar in minimal water

### Step 2: Add Flavors
- Add 10 mL Flavor Solution B
- Add 1 mL Flavor Solution A

### Step 3: Heat
- Heat until nearly boiling
- Cover immediately

### Step 4: Carbonate
- Cool completely
- Dilute with cold carbonated water to 1 liter

### Step 5: Rest
- Refrigerate 12–24 hours before drinking
